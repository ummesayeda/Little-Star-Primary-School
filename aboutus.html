Our Dedicated Staff

Meet our passionate and skilled teachers who inspire students every day.

Mrs. Emily Johnson

Science Group Teacher

Our Science Group Teacher is dedicated to sparking curiosity and nurturing a love for learning among students. With a strong academic background in physics, chemistry, and biology, they simplify complex concepts into engaging lessons that children can easily understand. By combining interactive experiments, real-life examples, and modern teaching methods, the teacher inspires students to think critically, explore creatively, and develop problem-solving skills. Beyond academics, they also encourage teamwork, discipline, and confidence, helping young learners grow into inquisitive and responsible individuals.

Mr.Joseph Barnard

General Group Teacher

Our General Group Teacher plays a vital role in building a strong foundation for students by teaching essential subjects such as language, mathematics, and social studies. With patience and creativity, the teacher ensures that every lesson is engaging, easy to understand, and tailored to different learning styles. They focus not only on academics but also on developing communication, problem-solving, and life skills that prepare children for future challenges. By creating a supportive classroom environment, the teacher inspires confidence, discipline, and a love for lifelong learning in every student.

Mrs. Emily Johnson

Art & Cultural Group Teacher

Our Art & Cultural Group Teacher is passionate about nurturing creativity and self-expression in young learners. Through painting, music, drama, and cultural activities, they inspire students to explore their talents and appreciate the beauty of diversity. With a balance of traditional practices and modern approaches, the teacher encourages imagination, teamwork, and confidence in every child. About Little Star Primary School

Little Star Primary School is dedicated to providing a nurturing and inspiring environment where children can grow academically, socially, and emotionally. With a focus on both traditional values and modern learning approaches, we ensure that every child receives a balanced education.

Our curriculum includes science, general education, and art & cultural studies, empowering students to explore their interests and develop essential life skills. Beyond academics, we emphasize creativity, teamwork, discipline, and cultural awareness, shaping well-rounded individuals ready for future challenges.

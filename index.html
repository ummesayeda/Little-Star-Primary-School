Welcome to Little Stars Primary School, where we believe in nurturing young minds with knowledge, discipline, and creativity. Our mission is to create a joyful and engaging learning environment that prepares children for a successful future. We are committed to academic excellence, moral values, and holistic growth. Together with our dedicated faculty and supportive community, we strive to inspire every student to reach their fullest potential.

Our Courses

We provide a variety of courses to develop knowledge, skills, and creativity among students.

General

The General course provides a balanced curriculum covering language, mathematics, social studies, and life skills. It is designed to strengthen overall knowledge and develop essential problem-solving and communication abilities.

Science

Our Science course encourages curiosity and discovery through hands-on experiments, critical thinking, and innovative learning methods. Students explore subjects like physics, chemistry, and biology to build a strong foundation for future academic success.

Art & Cultural

The Art & Culture course nurtures creativity and self-expression while introducing students to music, painting, drama, and cultural heritage. It helps children appreciate diversity and develop their artistic talents with confidence.
